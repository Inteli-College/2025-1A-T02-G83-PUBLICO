## The Complete Business Model

### Value Creation
We create value through our AI-powered creative strategy platform by:
- **Efficiency Gains**: Automating time-consuming creative processes
- **Performance Improvement**: Using AI to optimize ad creative campaigns
- **Cost Reduction**: Reducing the need for expensive creative resources
- **AI-Driven Insights**: Providing actionable analytics for better decision-making

### Value Delivery
We deliver this value through:
- **Unified Workspace**: Cloud-based platform where teams research, create, analyze, and scale ad creatives
- **AI Integration**: Advanced artificial intelligence for creative optimization
- **User Experience**: Intuitive interface designed for creative professionals and growth teams

### Value Capture
We capture value through our subscription-based pricing model:
- **Recurring Revenue**: Predictable monthly and annual subscriptions
- **Scalable Pricing**: Tiered plans that grow with customer needs
- **Premium Positioning**: Pricing that reflects the advanced value we provide
- **Expansion Revenue**: Upselling and cross-selling opportunities

## Revenue Model

### Subscription Tiers
Our simplified two-tier pricing structure provides complete access to all platform features:

#### Monthly Plan - R$ 139.00/month
**Target**: Users seeking flexibility and month-to-month commitment
- **Features**: Complete access to all functionalities
- **Value**: Full platform access with no long-term commitment
- **Conversion**: Trial conversions and flexible users
- **Billing**: Monthly recurring charges

#### Annual Plan - R$ 99.00/month (R$ 1,188.00/year)
**Target**: Committed users seeking maximum value and savings
- **Features**: Complete access to all functionalities
- **Value**: 30% savings compared to monthly plan
- **Conversion**: Monthly plan upgrades and value-conscious users
- **Billing**: Annual upfront payment
- **Popular Choice**: Highlighted as the preferred option

### Free Trial Strategy
**Target**: All potential users evaluating the platform
- **Features**: Full platform access for 3 days
- **Value**: Risk-free evaluation with no credit card required
- **Conversion**: Trial to paid subscription (monthly or annual)
- **Duration**: 3-day trial period

### Revenue Optimization
We maximize revenue through:
- **Annual Discounts**: 20% savings encourage longer commitments
- **Feature Upselling**: Introducing advanced capabilities to existing users
- **Plan Upgrades**: Moving users to higher-value tiers
- **Add-on Services**: Consulting, training, and custom development

## Key Metrics and KPIs

### Customer Acquisition
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: Cost to acquire a new customer
- **Conversion Rate**: Percentage of trials that become paid users
- **Lead Quality**: Engagement and qualification of LinkedIn leads
- **Channel Efficiency**: Performance of different acquisition channels

### Customer Success
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**: Total revenue per customer
- **Churn Rate**: Percentage of customers who cancel
- **Net Revenue Retention**: Revenue retention including expansions
- **Customer Satisfaction**: NPS and satisfaction scores

### Financial Performance
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)**: Predictable monthly revenue
- **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)**: Long-term revenue visibility
- **Gross Margin**: Profitability of our subscription model
- **Unit Economics**: CAC payback period and CLV/CAC ratio
